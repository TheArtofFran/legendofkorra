Staff

The co-creators of the show are Bryan Konietzko and Michael Dante DiMartino.

Studios involved in the production include in-house Nickelodeon and Netflix teams and outsourced staff from Studio Mir and Pierrot.

Joaquim Dos Santos is credited as background and character designer and director in "Avatar: The Last Airbender" and art director, character designer, director, executive producer, storyboard artist, and an additional voice in The Legend of Korra.

Ki Hyun Ryu is a television director of animated cartoons. He helped with character design for the episode "Jet" in Avatar: The Last Airbender and later returned for The Legend of Korra, directing every episode of Book One: Air. He has also worked as a character designer and supervising producer across all four books.

Lauren Montgomery is an American animation director and storyboard artist. She is credited as a storyboard artist for seven episodes of Book Three: Fire of Avatar: The Last Airbender and as a storyboard artist and supervising producer for the last three books of The Legend of Korra.
