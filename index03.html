CHARACTERS

Korra

Korra is the new avatar. She has been sheltered in the Southern Watertribe and is now moving to Republic City. She is impulsive and curious joined by her animal companion, Naga, a polar bear dog.

Tenzin

Tenzin is the youngest son of Aang and oldest airbending master. He is a serious man still learning to temper his own anger. He mentors Korra upon her arrival to Republic City.

Mako

Mako is a firebender and leader of the Fire Ferrets. He is a native of Republic City and orphan along side his brother, Bolin. He is often seen wearing his signature red scarf, a gift from his father.

Bolin

Bolin is an earthbender and a probender of the Fire Ferret team. He is often seen with his fire ferret, Pabu. His personality is bubbly and easygoing.

Asami is an intelligent engineer and entrepreneur of Future Industries, a company founded by her father, Hiroshi Sato. She is a nonbender skilled in hand-to-hand combat and designed her weapon of choice, an electrified glove.
